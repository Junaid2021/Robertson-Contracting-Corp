Our Story

Family-owned and operated with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction

Three Generations of Excellence in Contracting

Robertson Contracting Corp is a family-run contracting business with over 28 years of experience serving Bedford Hills, Mt Kisco, Katonah, and surrounding areas in New York. What started as a small excavation operation has grown into a full-service contracting company known for quality craftsmanship and personalized service.

Our mission is to provide efficient, reliable, and precise contracting services for projects of all sizes - from site preparation and excavating to complete home renovations and RB construction. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients through transparent communication, attention to detail, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

When you choose Robertson Contracting Corp, you're not just hiring a contractor - you're partnering with a dedicated team that treats your project with the same care and attention we would give our own homes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and process

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Robertson Contracting Corp is fully licensed, bonded, and insured for all excavating and contracting services in New York State. We can provide certificate of insurance upon request.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Bedford Hills, Mt Kisco, Katonah, North Salem, South Salem, and surrounding communities in Westchester County. We also take on select projects in neighboring counties.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small excavating jobs may take a few days, while full home renovations can take several months. We provide a detailed timeline during our consultation and keep you informed of progress throughout the project.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits, approvals, and coordinate with local building inspectors. We ensure all work complies with local building codes and regulations.
